About ZBN

We are here to help you succeed! ZBN Tax and Accounting Services was officially registered with CIPC in 2011. Our registration number is 2011/090027/23, and we trade as ZBN Tax and Accounting Services.

We are a 100% Black youth-owned enterprise, with 50% women ownership. ZBN is registered with SARS for Income Tax and with the National Treasury’s Central Supplier Database (CSD), ensuring full regulatory compliance.

Our offices are located in the Central Business District of KwaDukuza, KwaZulu-Natal, at 112 King Shaka Street, Stanger Centre, Office No. 1 & 29.

As the demand for support among SMMEs grows, so does our commitment. We are proud to be a fast-growing, reliable partner for small businesses.
